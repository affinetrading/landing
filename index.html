Affine Trading

Decentralized exchanges have come a long way since the early days of EtherDelta. Today, DEXs account for more than 20% of total crypto trading volume—driven by the rapid rise of platforms like Jupiter and Hyperliquid. Yet, despite this progress, decentralized markets still lag behind centralized exchanges (CEXs) when it comes to asset coverage and pricing efficiency.

Affine Trading was founded to bridge that gap. We focus on market making within DeFi markets, helping align DEX pricing with CEX benchmarks and driving liquidity where it’s needed most. Our mission is to accelerate the decentralization of global trading infrastructure while improving the trading experience for all market participants.
